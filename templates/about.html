About Us

Acme Digitek Solutions was established in 1992 by the name and style of Digitek Systems. It started as a repair and maintenance organization doing repair work and Third Party Maintenance services. Later it expanded into Sales, Networking, Infrastructure, and IT Services.

Over the years, Digitek partnered with leading global companies like HP, Cisco, and Sun Microsystems. Today it is a leading Systems Integration company delivering innovative IT solutions across India.
